  AI Orders

The following are valid player-issued communication orders (OPF_AI_ORDER) that can be passed to SEXPs like set-player-orders or set-order-allowed-for-target. These exact strings represent the orders that the player can issue via the communication menu.

*Attack Target*
    Orders the ship or wing to attack the player's current target.
*Disable Target*
    Orders the ship or wing to destroy the engine subsystems of the target.
*Disarm Target*
    Orders the ship or wing to destroy the turret subsystems of the target.
*Destroy Subsystem*
    Orders the ship or wing to attack the currently selected subsystem.
*Protect Target*
    Orders the ship or wing to protect the player's current target.
*Ignore Target*
    Orders the ship or wing to ignore the player's current target.
*Form on my wing*
    Orders the ship or wing to form up on the player's wing.
*Cover me*
    Orders the ship or wing to protect the player.
*Engage Enemy*
    Orders the ship or wing to attack any hostile target.
*Capture Target*
    Orders the ship to dock with and capture the target.
*Rearm me*
    Orders a support ship to rearm and repair the player.
*Abort rearm*
    Orders the support ship to abort the rearm operation.
*Depart*
    Orders the ship or wing to warp out of the mission.
