# Campaign Bible: Vessels of Corruption

## Overview
"Vessels of Corruption" is a 12-mission Freespace 1 campaign set during the final weeks of the Great War (2335). It focuses on a secret GTI task force investigating a "subspace anomaly" that is effectively a breach in the fabric of reality, through which the Shivans are not just invading, but *infecting* the Terran fleet.

## Timeline Placement
- **Date**: October 2335 - December 2335.
- **Context**: The GTD Galatea has been destroyed. Vasuda Prime is being bombarded or has already fallen. The GTA is desperate.
- **Location**: The Adhara system and an unstable, uncharted node leading to "The Void" (Subspace Pocket 09-X).

## Factions and Order of Battle
- **GTA Special Operations Command (SOC)**: The player's unit. High-tech, experimental, but isolated.
- **GTD Erebus (Orion-class)**: The player's carrier. A ship that has seen too much combat; its corridors are dark, its crew is on the edge of psychosis.
- **GTI "Project Necromancer"**: A rogue division of GTI that believes Shivan biology can be used to "save" humanity by merging with it.
- **The Infected**: Terran and Vasudan ships (Fenris, Leviathan, Aten) that have been overgrown with Shivan bio-mechanical structures.
- **The Shivans**: Depicted not as a military force, but as an eldritch, multi-dimensional entity that views the 3D universe as a feeding ground.

## Themes and Narrative Arc
- **Cosmic Horror**: The realization that the Great War is just a "harvest" and humanity is the crop.
- **Body Horror (Mechanical)**: Ships are treated as bodies; the "infection" manifests as pulsating hulls, screaming communications, and turrets that look like limbs.
- **Isolation**: The player is cut off from Command for much of the campaign.
- **The Twist**: The "Project Necromancer" leaders aren't villains in the traditional sense; they are the first to be fully "re-written" by the Shivans, acting as puppets to ensure the infection reaches Earth.

## Systems and Node Routes
- **Adhara**: The primary base of operations.
- **The Void (09-X)**: An unstable pocket of subspace accessed via a hidden node in Adhara. This is where the "Singularity" resides.

## Era Mechanics and Constraints
- **Ships**: GTF Apollo, GTF Valkyrie, GTF Loki, GTB Medusa, GTB Ursa.
- **Weapons**: ML-16, Avenger, Flail, Prometheus.
- **No Beam Weapons**: Strictly FS1 era.
- **Shields**: Standard Shivan/Terran shield rules apply.

## Plotline Summary
- **Act 1: The Silence**: Investigating the disappearance of the GTSC Styx in Adhara. Discovery of the first "Infected" ships. The horror of what happened to the crew.
- **Act 2: The Singularity**: Venturing into the Void. Realizing GTI is complicit. The psychological toll on the wingmen. The discovery that the "Lucifer" isn't the only one of its kind; it's just the tip of the spear.
- **Act 3: The Protocol**: The infection attempts to jump to Delta Serpentis to reach Sol. The player must execute "Protocol Omega"—the total destruction of the infected fleet, even if it means killing thousands of uninfected refugees to ensure the "spores" don't reach Earth.

## Continuity Notes
- This campaign takes place in parallel to the final missions of FS1. While the Bastion is chasing the Lucifer, the Erebus is fighting a "silent war" to prevent a biological catastrophe.
- It explains why the GTI was so obsessed with Shivan tech in "Silent Threat"—they were already seeing the "corruption" and trying to weaponize it.
- The "Void" node collapses at the end of the campaign, ensuring it doesn't contradict the known map of 2367.

## Mission List

### Act 1: The Silence
*   **Mission 1: Ghost Signal**
    *   **Date**: 24 Oct 2335 | **System**: Adhara
    *   **Type**: Recon / Asteroid Sweep
    *   **Objectives**: P: Inspect debris of GTSC Styx; S: Destroy Shivan scouts; B: Scan unusual cargo containers.
    *   **Narrative**: The first hint that something is "wrong." The cargo containers are pulsing.
    *   **Introductions**: GTF Loki (Recon).
*   **Mission 2: Triage**
    *   **Date**: 25 Oct 2335 | **System**: Adhara
    *   **Type**: Escort / Defense
    *   **Objectives**: P: Defend GTSC Faustus "Lifeblood"; S: Defend medical escape pods.
    *   **Narrative**: Rescuing survivors from the GTC Aethelgard. Comms from the survivors are gibberish and screaming.
*   **Mission 3: Outbreak**
    *   **Date**: 27 Oct 2335 | **System**: Adhara
    *   **Type**: Disable / Capture
    *   **Objectives**: P: Disable GTC Aethelgard's engines; S: Disarm turrets; B: Prevent the cruiser from jumping.
    *   **Narrative**: The Aethelgard has gone rogue. The hull is visibly changing color.
*   **Mission 4: The Styx Found**
    *   **Date**: 30 Oct 2335 | **System**: Adhara (Unstable Node)
    *   **Type**: Search and Destroy
    *   **Objectives**: P: Destroy the GTSC Styx; S: Eliminate Shivan escorts.
    *   **Narrative**: The Styx is no longer a ship; it's a hive. High Command orders its total liquidation.
    *   **Fiction Viewer**: After Mission 4: A description of the "Wrongness" seen on the Styx.

### Act 2: The Singularity
*   **Mission 5: Into the Void**
    *   **Date**: 05 Nov 2335 | **System**: The Void (Subspace Pocket)
    *   **Type**: Exploration / Ambush
    *   **Objectives**: P: Protect GTD Erebus during transit; S: Identify Shivan "Bio-Spires."
    *   **Narrative**: Entering a pocket of subspace where reality is thin. The background is a swirling, visceral red.
    *   **Introductions**: GTW Banshee (Shield killer).
*   **Mission 6: Necromancer's Hand**
    *   **Date**: 07 Nov 2335 | **System**: The Void
    *   **Type**: Strike / Intel Gathering
    *   **Objectives**: P: Destroy GTI "Black Lab" facility; S: Scan data cores; B: Capture rogue GTI scientist.
    *   **Narrative**: Discovering that GTI has been experimenting with Shivan "corruption" on human subjects.
*   **Mission 7: The Whispering Dark**
    *   **Date**: 10 Nov 2335 | **System**: The Void
    *   **Type**: Stealth Recon
    *   **Objectives**: P: Scan the SD Malphas (Demon-class); S: Remain undetected; B: Locate the source of the "Whisper" signal.
    *   **Narrative**: The player hears voices over the comms that aren't there. The SD Malphas is literally breathing.
*   **Mission 8: Internal Bleeding**
    *   **Date**: 15 Nov 2335 | **System**: The Void
    *   **Type**: Red Alert / Point Defense
    *   **Objectives**: P: Defend GTD Erebus fighterbays; S: Destroy "Breach Pods."
    *   **Narrative**: The Shivans have bypassed shields and are launching organic pods directly into the Erebus's hull.
    *   **Fiction Viewer**: Before Mission 8: A pilot's journal entry about the voices in the walls.

### Act 3: The Protocol
*   **Mission 9: The Harvest Moon**
    *   **Date**: 25 Nov 2335 | **System**: Adhara (Planetary Orbit)
    *   **Type**: Planetary Defense / Interdiction
    *   **Objectives**: P: Intercept Shivan "Seeds"; S: Protect orbital hab-units.
    *   **Narrative**: Giant organic spores are being dropped onto the colony. Total horror on the surface.
    *   **Introductions**: GTB Ursa (Heavy Bomber) & Harbinger Bomb.
*   **Mission 10: Treason of the Soul**
    *   **Date**: 30 Nov 2335 | **System**: Adhara
    *   **Type**: Fleet Engagement
    *   **Objectives**: P: Destroy GTC infected cruisers; S: Defend the Erebus; B: Destroy the GTI "Director's" ship.
    *   **Narrative**: The GTI fleet openly defects to the "corruption," appearing as fused bio-mechanical monstrosities.
*   **Mission 11: Protocol Omega**
    *   **Date**: 05 Dec 2335 | **System**: Adhara-Delta Serpentis Node
    *   **Type**: Moral Dilemma / Surgical Strike
    *   **Objectives**: P: Destroy the refugee convoy; S: Prevent any ship from jumping to Delta Serpentis.
    *   **Narrative**: The most tragic mission. The refugees are infected. If they reach Earth, it's over.
*   **Mission 12: The Final Seal**
    *   **Date**: 07 Dec 2335 | **System**: The Singularity (Void Core)
    *   **Type**: Capital Ship Assault / Suicide Run
    *   **Objectives**: P: Destroy the Shivan "Core Destroyer"; S: Collateral damage to the Singularity Anchor.
    *   **Narrative**: Destroying the anchor will collapse the Void node, trapping the Erebus but saving Sol.
    *   **Fiction Viewer**: Epilogue: A final transmission from the Erebus into the dark.
