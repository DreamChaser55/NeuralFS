# Fiction Viewer: Epilogue - The Silent Sentinel

This is the final automated transmission from the GTD Erebus, broadcasted on all frequencies into the Adhara system. 

The rift is closed. The Singularity Anchor has been shattered, and the Void node has collapsed into a point of zero-mass. We are on the other side now. The white light of the core is fading, replaced by a darkness so absolute it feels like a physical weight against the hull. The SD Malphas is gone, torn apart by the same tidal forces that have crippled this ship.

Admiral Kress is dead. Most of the crew followed him when the life support failed an hour ago. I am sitting in the flight deck, watching the frost crawl across the monitors. Outside, there are no stars. No planets. Just the whispering. It’s louder now that the fighting has stopped. It’s almost... peaceful.

I know what we did. I know the cost of the fire we brought to the Adhara colony. I know the names of the three thousand people we burned at the jump node. I hope history forgets us. I hope it remembers only that the door was shut. 

If anyone ever finds this, tell Earth that the stars are still there. Even if we can’t see them anymore. We are the final seal. We are the watchmen in the dark.

Signing off.
- Lieutenant Sarah Vane, GTD Erebus.
