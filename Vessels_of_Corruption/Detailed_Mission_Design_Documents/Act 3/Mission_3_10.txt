# Mission Design Document: Treason of the Soul (3.10)

## Purpose and Player Experience
- **Mission Fantasy**: A massive fleet engagement that turns into a nightmare when half the friendly fleet mutinies. The player feels the shock of betrayal.
- **Plot Advancement**: The rogue GTI division (Project Necromancer) openly sides with the Shivans. The "Infected" GTI ships attack the Erebus.
- **Tone**: Brutal, chaotic, and high-stakes.

## Environment and Setup
- **Datestamp**: 30 Nov 2335
- **System**: Adhara
- **Location**: Near the Adhara-Sirius jump node.
- **Backgrounds**: Starfield with the glowing debris of the Adhara Orbital Hub.
- **Hazards**: Crossfire between massive capital ships. Erratic EMP bursts from infected cruisers.

## Participants
- **Allied**:
    - **GTD Erebus (Orion-class)**: The Player's Carrier. Status: 70% Hull. 
    - **GTC Orff (Fenris-class)**: Loyal cruiser escort.
    - **Alpha Wing (Player)**: 4x GTF Hercules.
    - **Beta Wing**: 4x GTF Valkyrie.
- **Hostile/Neutral**:
    - **GTC Reaper (Infected Fenris)**: Rogue GTI ship. Hull is covered in black, pulsating Shivan matter.
    - **GTC Nightmare (Infected Leviathan)**: Rogue GTI ship.
    - **GTD Myrmidon (Orion-class)**: Rogue GTI Destroyer (Arrives later).
    - **Bragi Wing**: 2 wings of 4 GTF Loki (Augmented GTI).
    - **Brahma Wing**: 3x SF Scorpion (Shivan support for GTI).
    - **Durga Wing**: 2x SB Shaitan (Shivan bombers).

## Player Craft and Loadout
- **Default Ship**: GTF Hercules.
- **Allowed Ships**: GTF Hercules, GTF Apollo, GTB Athena.
- **Default Loadout**: GTW Prometheus (Introduced), GTW-15 Avenger, GTM Interceptor.

## Objectives and Directives
- **Primary**:
    - Defend the GTD Erebus from the GTI attack.
    - Destroy the GTC Reaper and GTC Nightmare.
    - Prevent the GTD Myrmidon from ramming the Erebus.
- **Secondary**:
    - Eliminate the augmented GTI fighter wings (Bragi).
- **Bonus**:
    - Scan the GTC Reaper's bridge.

## Command Briefing
- **Stage 1**: The GTI's "Project Necromancer" has officially broken away from the Alliance. Admiral Kress, the lead of the division, has declared that humanity's only hope is to "ascend" through Shivan biology.
- **Stage 2**: The GTI fleet in Adhara is now openly hostile. These ships are no longer purely Terran; they have been modified with Shivan technologies. They are attacking our blockade at the Sirius node.
- **Stage 3**: This is a civil war in the middle of a Shivan incursion. The Erebus is being targeted as the primary obstacle to the GTI's "Ascension." We must destroy these traitors.

## Mission Briefing
- **Stage 1**: Alpha, the situation is grim. The GTC Reaper and GTC Nightmare are moving to intercept the Erebus. They're firing some kind of biological pulse that's shorting out our shields.
- **Stage 2**: Target the Reaper first. It's faster and more aggressive. Use the new Prometheus cannon—it's the only thing we've found that can effectively slice through the hardened bio-hull of these traitors.
- **Stage 3**: Watch out for the GTI pilots. They've been "augmented." They're faster and more resilient than any human pilot you've faced. Show them no mercy.

## In-mission Communications
- **GTC Reaper Captain**: "Do you see it now, Erebus? The perfection of the hive? Join us, or be recycled into the new world!"
- **Alpha 2 (Vane)**: "The Reaper... its turrets look like teeth. It's chewing through the Orff's armor!"
- **Erebus Command**: "Alpha wing, target the Nightmare's missile banks! They're launching Stilettos at our engines!"
- **Alpha 3 (Kael)**: "I can't lock on to the Loki wing! Their hulls are absorbing my radar! They're like ghosts!"
- **The Whisper**: (Roaring volume) "THE... BLOOD... IS... THE... OIL... OF... THE... NEW... MACHINE."

## Event Flow and Timing
- **00:00**: Alpha and Beta wings are patrolling when the Reaper and Nightmare jump in 3km away.
- **01:30**: Bragi wing (GTI Lokis) engages Alpha. They use advanced maneuvers.
- **04:00**: The Erebus's shields are drained by a "Bio-Pulse" from the Nightmare.
- **06:00**: Durga wing (Shivan bombers) arrives to assist the GTI.
- **08:00**: The GTD Myrmidon (Infected Orion) jumps in 5km away, on a direct collision course for the Erebus.
- **10:00**: Player must lead a heavy strike on the Myrmidon's engines to stop the ramming attempt.

## Event Logic Sketch
- **Bio-Pulse**: A unique visual effect (purple lightning) that drains 10% shield energy every 20 seconds for all allied ships.
- **Betrayal Reveal**: GTI ships are initially 'Neutral' on radar until the first 15 seconds pass.

## New Ships/Weapons
- **GTW Prometheus**: High-damage hull killer.
- **Infected Terran Ships**: Fenris and Leviathan variants with Shivan textures and increased HP.

## Debriefing
- **Success**: The traitors are destroyed, but the Myrmidon's destruction nearly took the Erebus with it. We've lost a third of our fleet to this mutiny. But the path to Earth is still closed to them.
- **Failure**: The Erebus was lost. The GTI fleet has jumped to Sirius. They are heading for Sol, carrying the infection with them. Humanity's "Ascension" has begun.

## QA Notes
- This is a very chaotic mission. Ensure the player's wingmen are effective at drawing fire from the Loki wings.
- The Myrmidon's ramming speed should be slow enough to allow for an engine-kill strategy.
