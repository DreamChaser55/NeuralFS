# Mission Design Document: The Harvest Moon (3.9)

## Purpose and Player Experience
- **Mission Fantasy**: A desperate battle above a dying colony. The horror is no longer just in space; it's raining down on civilians. The player feels the weight of a planet's survival.
- **Plot Advancement**: The Shivans begin a "planetary harvest" on the Adhara colony. Introduces the Ursa bomber and Harbinger bombs as the only hope.
- **Tone**: Epic, tragic, and overwhelming. The sky is filled with falling spores.

## Environment and Setup
- **Datestamp**: 25 Nov 2335
- **System**: Adhara
- **Location**: Low orbit of Adhara Prime.
- **Backgrounds**: A massive, blue-green planet dominates the view. The atmosphere is filled with bright, glowing entry trails of Shivan "Seeds."
- **Hazards**: Atmospheric drag (visual shake), falling debris, planetary gravity (mission boundary).

## Participants
- **Allied**:
    - **GTD Erebus (Orion-class)**: Status: Heavily Damaged. Providing fighter coordination.
    - **Alpha Wing (Player)**: 4x GTB Ursa (Heavy Bombers).
    - **Beta Wing**: 4x GTF Hercules (Cover).
    - **Gamma Wing**: 3x GTF Valkyrie (Interceptor).
- **Hostile/Neutral**:
    - **Shivan "Seeds" (Modified ST Azrael)**: 6x Large organic pods. These are the primary targets. They are heading for the planet's surface.
    - **Shivan Seraphim (SB Seraphim)**: 2 waves of 2 (Rama wing). Heavy escort for the Seeds.
    - **Shivan Manticore (SF Manticore)**: 3 waves of 4 (Krishna wing).
    - **Adhara Orbital Hub**: Civilian installation under attack.

## Player Craft and Loadout
- **Default Ship**: GTB Ursa.
- **Allowed Ships**: GTB Ursa only (Harbinger requirement).
- **Default Loadout**: GTW-15 Avenger, GTW Flail, GTM Harbinger Bomb (Required), GTM Hornet Swarm.

## Objectives and Directives
- **Primary**:
    - Destroy all Shivan "Seeds" before they enter the lower atmosphere.
    - Protect the Adhara Orbital Hub.
- **Secondary**:
    - Eliminate the Shivan Seraphim bombers.
- **Bonus**:
    - Rescue the GTEP Hermes pods from the Orbital Hub.

## Command Briefing 3.1
- **Stage 1**: The GTD Erebus has returned to Adhara space, but we have brought the nightmare with us. The Shivan fleet, led by a second Demon-class destroyer, has launched a full-scale assault on the colony.
- **Stage 2**: They are deploying "Seeds"—colossal organic spores—into Adhara Prime's atmosphere. Preliminary analysis suggests these Seeds contain biological agents designed to liquefy the planet's ecosystem into a Shivan nutrient-sludge.
- **Stage 3**: We have one advantage. The GTI Project Ursa has delivered a wing of heavy bombers to the Erebus. These ships are capable of carrying the Harbinger nuclear bomb. You will use them to incinerate the Seeds before they can impact.

## Mission Briefing
- **Stage 1**: Alpha, the Seeds are large and heavily armored. Your standard torpedoes won't work. You'll need to use the Harbingers. They take time to lock, so keep your distance.
- **Stage 2**: The Shivans have deployed Seraphim bombers to protect the Seeds. These are the most dangerous bombers we've ever faced. Beta and Gamma will try to keep them off you.
- **Stage 3**: Adhara Prime has a population of four million. If even one Seed reaches the surface, the "Harvest" begins. Do not let that happen.

## In-mission Communications
- **Adhara Hub Control**: "They're raining down on us! The sky is turning purple! Erebus, help us!"
- **Alpha 1 (Player)**: (Silent).
- **Alpha 2 (Vane)**: "Locking on to the first Seed. It's... it's as big as a cruiser. Launching Harbinger!"
- **Erebus Command**: "Alpha wing, target the Seeds! The Seraphim are moving to intercept! Beta, engage those bombers!"
- **Alpha 3 (Kael)**: "Look at the planet. The trails... they look like scars on the sky."
- **The Whisper**: (Softly) "The... soil... is... ready. Let... us... plant."

## Event Flow and Timing
- **00:00**: Mission starts in high orbit. 6 Seeds are spread out in a V-formation, moving toward the planet.
- **02:00**: Rama wing (Seraphims) jumps in 5km behind Alpha wing.
- **04:00**: The first Seed reaches "Critical Altitude" (visual warning).
- **06:00**: Krishna wing (Manticores) attacks the Orbital Hub. Hub hull begins to drop.
- **08:00**: If a Seed is destroyed, a massive explosion illuminates the planet.
- **12:00**: Final Seed must be destroyed.

## Event Logic Sketch
- **Harbinger Lock**: The Harbinger requires a 10-second lock-on. The Seeds have high HP but zero shields.
- **Impact Fail**: If a Seed reaches < 1000m from the planet's "surface" boundary, the mission ends in failure (Cutscene of the planet turning black).

## New Ships/Weapons
- **GTB Ursa**: Heavy Bomber.
- **GTM Harbinger**: Nuclear yield bomb.
- **SB Seraphim**: Heavy Shivan Bomber.

## Debriefing
- **Success**: The sky over Adhara is clear of the Seeds, but the cost was high. The Orbital Hub is a wreck, but we saved the colony. For now. The infection in the fleet is still our primary concern.
- **Failure**: The Seeds have impacted. Reports from the surface are... horrific. The population is being converted. Adhara Prime is no longer a human world.

## QA Notes
- The Ursa is slow; the player must manage their energy to reach all 6 Seeds in time.
- The Harbingers have a large blast radius; ensure the player doesn't kill themselves.
