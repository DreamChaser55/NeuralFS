# Mission Design Document: Triage (1.2)

## Purpose and Player Experience
- **Mission Fantasy**: A desperate rescue mission where the "rescue" feels wrong. The survivors aren't grateful; they are terrified of the ships coming to save them.
- **Plot Advancement**: Introduces the psychological toll of the Shivan "whisper" and the first physical signs of the infection on survivors.
- **Tone**: Chaotic, tense, and disturbing. Audio-visual static.

## Environment and Setup
- **Datestamp**: 25 Oct 2335
- **System**: Adhara
- **Location**: Near the burning wreckage of the GTC Aethelgard (Fenris-class).
- **Backgrounds**: The debris field is illuminated by the flickering orange light of the Aethelgard's exposed reactor.
- **Hazards**: Unstable debris, localized EMP bursts from the dying cruiser.

## Participants
- **Allied**:
    - **Alpha Wing (Player)**: 4x GTF Apollo.
    - **Beta Wing**: 3x GTF Valkyrie (Interceptor cover).
    - **GTSC Lifeblood (Faustus-class)**: Science/Medical vessel sent to retrieve pods.
    - **Escape Pods**: 6x GTEP Hermes (from the Aethelgard).
- **Hostile/Neutral**:
    - **Shivan Shaitan (SB Shaitan)**: 2 waves of 3 (Durga wing). Target the pods.
    - **Shivan Scorpion (SF Scorpion)**: 3 waves of 4 (Rama wing). Target Alpha and Beta.
    - **The Aethelgard survivors**: (Inside the pods, non-combatant but vocal).

## Player Craft and Loadout
- **Default Ship**: GTF Apollo.
- **Allowed Ships**: GTF Apollo, GTF Valkyrie.
- **Default Loadout**: ML-16 Laser, GTW-15 Avenger, GTM MX-50.

## Objectives and Directives
- **Primary**:
    - Protect the GTSC Lifeblood during the recovery operation.
    - At least 4 escape pods must be recovered.
- **Secondary**:
    - Destroy all Shivan bombers (Durga wing).
- **Bonus**:
    - Scan the Aethelgard's bridge section for data logs.

## Command Briefing
- **Stage 1**: The GTC Aethelgard was ambushed while patrolling the Adhara-Sirius corridor. It didn't stand a chance. However, escape pods were launched before the hull was breached.
- **Stage 2**: The GTSC Lifeblood is on station to retrieve the survivors. We have reports of Shivan bombers in the vicinity. They aren't just destroying the pods; they seem to be "scanning" them before the kill.
- **Stage 3**: Your job is to provide a perimeter. The Lifeblood is a science ship, not a warship. Do not let anything get within 500 meters of it.

## Mission Briefing
- **Stage 1**: Alpha, you are the shield. Beta, you are the sword. The Lifeblood will move from pod to pod. Stay close to the science ship; its sensors are being jammed by the Aethelgard's wreckage.
- **Stage 2**: The Shivans are using "Shaitan" class bombers. These are heavily shielded. Use your Avengers to strip their shields before they can lock on to the pods.
- **Stage 3**: We're picking up unusual transmissions from the pods. High-frequency static and... what sounds like singing. Ignore it. Focus on the mission.

## In-mission Communications
- **Lifeblood Captain**: "Alpha 1, we're moving to the first cluster. Sensors are a mess. It's like the vacuum is screaming."
- **Escape Pod 3**: (Audio) "It sees us. The black sun. It's inside the walls. Don't let them take us back to the Erebus!"
- **Alpha 2 (Vane)**: "Did you hear that, Alpha 1? Pod 3 sounds... insane. What did they see in there?"
- **Erebus Command**: "Alpha wing, focus on the hostiles. Do not engage with survivor comms. That is an order."
- **Shivan Bombers (Durga)**: (Arrive with a distinctive, low-pitched subspace distortion sound).

## Event Flow and Timing
- **00:00**: Alpha and Lifeblood arrive. The Aethelgard is a twisted ruin.
- **01:30**: First wave of Scorpions (Rama 1) attacks from the nebula.
- **03:00**: Lifeblood begins docking with Pod 1. Durga 1 (Shaitans) jumps in 3km away, heading for Pod 2.
- **05:00**: Lifeblood Captain: "We've got the survivors from Pod 1. They're... they're physically intact, but they're clawing at the bulkheads. We're sedating them."
- **07:00**: Final wave of bombers. If pods are lost, the Captain's tone becomes increasingly panicked.
- **09:00**: Lifeblood completes recovery and jumps out.

## Event Logic Sketch
- **Pod Protection**: If a pod is destroyed, the mission continues unless 3 pods are lost.
- **Distraction**: Shivan Scorpions will try to lure the player away from the Lifeblood, allowing the Shaitans to sneak in from the opposite side.

## Debriefing
- **Success**: The Lifeblood is safe. The survivors are being moved to the Erebus's high-security medical wing. Command is... concerned about their mental state. They keep talking about a "singularity."
- **Failure**: The survivors are gone. Whatever secrets they held died with them. The Adhara system is a graveyard, and we're just adding to the body count.

## QA Notes
- The Shaitans are slow; the player should have enough time to intercept them if they are paying attention.
- Ensure the "gibberish" comms are unsettling but don't drown out the tactical info.
