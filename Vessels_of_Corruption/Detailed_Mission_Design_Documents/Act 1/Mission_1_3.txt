# Mission Design Document: Outbreak (1.3)

## Purpose and Player Experience
- **Mission Fantasy**: A frantic pursuit of a friendly ship that is no longer friendly. The player must choose between duty (disabling the ship) and fear (what is happening to it).
- **Plot Advancement**: First direct combat encounter with an "Infected" capital ship. Reveals that the Shivan "corruption" can hijack Terran technology.
- **Tone**: High-intensity, tragic, and disturbing. The ship's "screaming" engines.

## Environment and Setup
- **Datestamp**: 27 Oct 2335
- **System**: Adhara
- **Location**: Deep space, heading toward the Adhara-Sirius jump node.
- **Backgrounds**: Dark, with the distant glint of the Adhara star.
- **Hazards**: The Aethelgard is venting radioactive gas (visual effect) and erratic turret fire.

## Participants
- **Allied**:
    - **Alpha Wing (Player)**: 4x GTF Apollo.
    - **Beta Wing**: 3x GTB Athena (equipped with Disruptor missiles).
    - **GTC Orff (Fenris-class)**: Distant, providing tactical oversight.
- **Hostile/Neutral**:
    - **GTC Aethelgard (Fenris-class)**: The Target. Status: Infected. Hull has dark, pulsating patches. Turrets fire randomly.
    - **Shivan Manticore (SF Manticore)**: 2 waves of 3 (Brahma wing). Guarding the Aethelgard.
    - **Shivan Shaitan (SB Shaitan)**: 1 wave of 2 (Durga wing). Attempting to "push" the Aethelgard into subspace.

## Player Craft and Loadout
- **Default Ship**: GTF Apollo.
- **Allowed Ships**: GTF Apollo, GTF Valkyrie, GTB Athena.
- **Default Loadout**: ML-16 Laser, GTW-15 Avenger, GTW Disruptor (Required for engines).

## Objectives and Directives
- **Primary**:
    - Disable the engines of the GTC Aethelgard.
    - Disarm the Aethelgard's primary turrets.
    - Prevent the Aethelgard from reaching the jump node.
- **Secondary**:
    - Destroy the Shivan escort (Brahma wing).
- **Bonus**:
    - Minimize damage to the Aethelgard's hull (keep above 40%).

## Command Briefing
- **Stage 1**: At 0600 hours, the GTC Aethelgard—or what's left of it—re-ignited its engines and broke orbit. It is currently on a direct heading for the Sirius jump node.
- **Stage 2**: We have attempted to contact the ship, but the only response is a continuous loop of what sounds like the crew screaming in unison. High Command has declared the ship rogue.
- **Stage 3**: You are to intercept. Do not destroy the ship unless absolutely necessary. We need to capture it and find out how the Shivans took control of a Terran cruiser from the inside.

## Mission Briefing
- **Stage 1**: Alpha, the Aethelgard is making 30 meters per second. You'll need to use your afterburners to close the distance. Beta will provide the heavy disruption fire.
- **Stage 2**: Target the engine subsystem first. Once the ship is dead in the water, take out its turrets. Be careful—the ship's AI is compromised and will view you as a hostile.
- **Stage 3**: We're picking up Shivan signatures jumping in around the Aethelgard. It seems they want their prize to reach Sirius. Do not let that happen.

## In-mission Communications
- **Alpha 2 (Vane)**: "My god, Alpha 1... look at the hull. It looks like... scales. It's growing."
- **Erebus Command**: "Alpha wing, target the engines! The Aethelgard is at 5000 meters from the node. Move it!"
- **Aethelgard (Automated)**: "Biological... contamination... detected. Purging... internal... atmosphere. Help... us..." (Followed by static).
- **Beta 1**: "Tsunami's away! Wait, the Disruptors aren't working! The engines are regenerating!"
- **Erebus Command**: "Keep firing, Beta! You have to overheat the reactor!"

## Event Flow and Timing
- **00:00**: Alpha and Beta wings jump in 4km behind the Aethelgard.
- **01:00**: First wave of Shivans (Brahma 1) jumps in to block the player.
- **03:00**: If Aethelgard is not disabled, it increases speed. Hull starts to pulsate red.
- **05:00**: The Aethelgard begins to "charge" its jump drive (visual flare).
- **06:30**: Final window to disable engines. If failed, Aethelgard jumps and mission fails.
- **08:00**: Once disabled, a GTI capture transport (GTT Valkyrie "Extraction") arrives.

## Event Logic Sketch
- **Engine Regen**: The Aethelgard's engines will "heal" back to 10% if the player stops firing for more than 30 seconds, forcing constant pressure.
- **Jump Timer**: A hidden timer starts when the Aethelgard is 2km from the node.

## New Ships/Weapons
- **GTW Disruptor**: Crucial for disabling.

## Debriefing
- **Success**: The Aethelgard is disabled. Our capture teams are reporting that the interior is... unrecognizable. The corridors are filled with a black, oily substance. But we have the ship.
- **Failure**: The Aethelgard reached the node. If that thing reaches a populated system, the infection will spread. You had one job, Alpha.

## QA Notes
- The "regeneration" mechanic should be clearly messaged to the player so they don't think it's a bug.
- Ensure the turrets are dangerous enough to require "disarming" but not so powerful they insta-kill the player.
