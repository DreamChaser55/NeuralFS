# Mission Design Document: Internal Bleeding (2.8)

## Purpose and Player Experience
- **Mission Fantasy**: A frantic, claustrophobic defense of the player's home. The battle isn't just in space; it's *on* the carrier.
- **Plot Advancement**: The Shivans launch a direct boarding assault on the GTD Erebus using organic "Breach Pods." The infection reaches the carrier's interior.
- **Tone**: Panic, desperation, and gritty defense. The sound of metal tearing.

## Environment and Setup
- **Datestamp**: 15 Nov 2335
- **System**: The Void
- **Location**: Orbiting the GTD Erebus.
- **Backgrounds**: The red Void nebula. The Erebus is visibly damaged, with fires burning on its hull.
- **Hazards**: Debris from the Erebus's outer plating. Friendly fire from the Erebus's own panicked turrets.

## Participants
- **Allied**:
    - **GTD Erebus (Orion-class)**: The Target. Status: Under Assault. It has "Breach Points" on the hull where the pods are attaching.
    - **Alpha Wing (Player)**: 4x GTF Hercules.
    - **Beta Wing**: 3x GTF Valkyrie (Defending the fighterbay).
    - **Support Ship**: GTS Charon (Distant, unable to dock due to fire).
- **Hostile/Neutral**:
    - **Breach Pods (Custom ST Azrael)**: 4 waves of 4. These are fast, unshielded organic pods that attempt to ram the Erebus's hull.
    - **Shivan Scorpion (SF Scorpion)**: 3 waves of 4 (Brahma wing).
    - **Shivan Manticore (SF Manticore)**: 2 waves of 3 (Krishna wing).

## Player Craft and Loadout
- **Default Ship**: GTF Hercules.
- **Allowed Ships**: GTF Hercules, GTF Apollo.
- **Default Loadout**: GTW-15 Avenger, GTW Flail, GTM Hornet Swarm (for pod interception).

## Objectives and Directives
- **Primary**:
    - Protect the GTD Erebus from Breach Pods.
    - Destroy all Shivan fighters (Brahma and Krishna wings).
    - At least 60% of the Erebus's hull must remain intact.
- **Secondary**:
    - Protect the Erebus's primary communications array.
- **Bonus**:
    - Intercept all pods in the 3rd wave before they impact.

## Command Briefing
- **Stage 1**: Red Alert! The SD Malphas has launched a direct assault on the Erebus. They aren't using bombers—they're using high-speed boarding pods.
- **Stage 2**: These "Breach Pods" are filled with Shivan ground troops and biological spores. If they penetrate the hull, we lose the ship from the inside out.
- **Stage 3**: All available fighters are to be scrambled immediately. You are the last line of defense. If the Erebus falls, we are all dead.

## Mission Briefing (Red Alert - Direct to Hangar)
- **Stage 1**: Alpha, scramble! The pods are already coming in. They're targeting the weaker hull sections near the engine block and the bridge.
- **Stage 2**: These pods are fast but have no shields. Use your Hornet missiles to take them out at range. Do not let them touch the hull.
- **Stage 3**: We're already picking up reports of breaches on Deck 12. Security is engaging, but we can't take many more hits. Go!

## In-mission Communications
- **Erebus Security**: "Breach on Deck 4! They're in the vents! They're... oh god, they're not using guns, they're using needles!"
- **Alpha 2 (Vane)**: "I've got a lock on the first group. There are too many of them, Alpha 1!"
- **Erebus Command**: "Alpha wing, focus on the pods! Ignore the Scorpions if you have to! The bridge is under attack!"
- **Alpha 3 (Kael)**: "I can hear them through the hull. They're scratching. They're scratching at the cockpit!"
- **The Whisper**: (Overwhelming volume) "Open... the... door. Let... the... light... in."

## Event Flow and Timing
- **00:00**: Alpha wing scrambles from the Erebus. The ship is surrounded by 4 pods already.
- **02:00**: Wave 2 of pods arrives from the 'bottom' of the Erebus.
- **04:00**: Brahma wing (Scorpions) engages Alpha to distract them.
- **06:00**: Wave 3 (The "Swarm") – 8 pods arrive simultaneously from multiple vectors.
- **08:00**: Krishna wing (Manticores) attacks the communications array.
- **10:00**: The last pod is destroyed or impacts. The Erebus manages to jump back to Adhara space.

## Event Logic Sketch
- **Impact Logic**: Each pod that impacts the Erebus does 5% hull damage and triggers a "Breach" audio message.
- **Kamikaze Pods**: Pods are set to 'Dock' with the Erebus, which in FRED terms means they fly directly at the hull and vanish on impact.

## New Ships/Weapons
- **Breach Pods**: High-speed, unshielded targets.

## Debriefing
- **Success**: We've escaped the Void, but the Erebus is a mess. We have Shivan organisms in the ventilation systems and half the crew is in quarantine. But we're back in Adhara. The final phase is beginning.
- **Failure**: The Erebus was boarded and over-run. The last transmission from the bridge was a single word: "Beautiful." The Void has claimed us all.

## QA Notes
- This is a high-speed interception mission. Ensure the Hercules' speed is sufficient or allow the player to switch to the Valkyrie.
- The pods should be the priority; the Scorpions are just there to add pressure.
