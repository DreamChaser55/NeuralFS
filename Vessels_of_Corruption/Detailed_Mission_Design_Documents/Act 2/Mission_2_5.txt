# Mission Design Document: Into the Void (2.5)

## Purpose and Player Experience
- **Mission Fantasy**: A terrifying transition into an alien dimension. The player feels the sheer "wrongness" of the environment—the physics, the colors, and the sounds are all distorted.
- **Plot Advancement**: The GTD Erebus enters "The Void," a pocket of subspace where the Shivans are cultivating their bio-mechanical infection.
- **Tone**: Surreal, claustrophobic, and high-tension. A "descent into hell" vibe.

## Environment and Setup
- **Datestamp**: 05 Nov 2335
- **System**: The Void (Subspace Pocket 09-X)
- **Location**: The threshold of the subspace anomaly.
- **Backgrounds**: A swirling, visceral red and purple nebula that pulses like a heartbeat. No stars are visible, only distant, glowing Shivan structures.
- **Hazards**: "Subspace Bleed" – Periodic sensor blackouts and minor hull damage (1% per minute) due to the corrosive nature of the pocket.

## Participants
- **Allied**:
    - **GTD Erebus (Orion-class)**: The carrier. Its shields are flickering and its hull is covered in frost.
    - **Alpha Wing (Player)**: 4x GTF Hercules.
    - **Beta Wing**: 3x GTF Valkyrie.
    - **Support Ship**: GTS Charon (Available).
- **Hostile/Neutral**:
    - **Shivan Dragon (SF Dragon)**: 1 wave of 1 (Arjuna 1). Appears briefly as a ghost on sensors before jumping out.
    - **Shivan Manticore (SF Manticore)**: 3 waves of 4 (Brahma wing).
    - **Shivan Basilisk (SF Basilisk)**: 2 waves of 3 (Krishna wing).
    - **Bio-Spires**: 3x Floating organic structures (equivalent to sentry guns) guarding the entry point.

## Player Craft and Loadout
- **Default Ship**: GTF Hercules.
- **Allowed Ships**: GTF Hercules, GTF Apollo, GTF Valkyrie.
- **Default Loadout**: GTW Banshee (Introduced), GTW-15 Avenger, GTM Hornet Swarm.

## Objectives and Directives
- **Primary**:
    - Protect the GTD Erebus during the 10-minute transit period.
    - Destroy the 3 Bio-Spires guarding the entry point.
- **Secondary**:
    - Identify (scan) the 3 Bio-Spires.
- **Bonus**:
    - Protect all ships in Beta wing.

## Command Briefing
- **Stage 1**: Following the destruction of the Styx hive, we have successfully stabilized the anomaly's core. The GTD Erebus is prepared to make the jump into the pocket we've designated "The Void."
- **Stage 2**: We are entering unknown territory. This pocket appears to be a Shivan nursery or shipyard, disconnected from the standard subspace network. Our goal is to locate the source of the infection.
- **Stage 3**: Upon arrival, expect the environment to be hostile. Your ship's computer has been updated with the latest sensor dampening filters, but expect glitches.

## Mission Briefing
- **Stage 1**: This is it, Alpha. We're jumping the Erebus directly into the anomaly. We'll be blind for the first sixty seconds of transit.
- **Stage 2**: Once we emerge, Alpha will fly point. We've detected three static signatures near the entry point. They look like the Spires we saw on the Styx. Take them out before the Erebus's shields can be targeted.
- **Stage 3**: Intelligence is issuing a new weapon for this mission—the Banshee laser. It's designed to puncture Shivan shields more effectively. You'll need it out there.

## In-mission Communications
- **Alpha 1 (Player)**: (Comm silence during jump).
- **Alpha 2 (Vane)**: "I... I think I'm going to be sick. The stars... they're gone. It's just red. Everything is red."
- **Erebus Command**: "All hands, brace for impact. We've emerged. Scramble Alpha and Beta! We have hostiles on scope!"
- **Alpha 3 (Kael)**: "Look at the Spires. They're not just turrets, they're... umbilical cords. They're feeding something."
- **Arjuna 1 (Dragon)**: (Briefly appears 5km away, scans the Erebus, and jumps out).
- **Alpha 2**: "Did you see that? It just... watched us."

## Event Flow and Timing
- **00:00**: Mission starts mid-jump. High-intensity red light and static.
- **01:00**: Emergence. The Erebus is 5km from the Spires. Brahma wing (Manticores) attacks immediately.
- **03:00**: Player must scan and destroy the Spires. They fire rapid-pulse green plasma.
- **05:00**: Krishna wing (Basilisks) jumps in from "above" the Erebus.
- **07:00**: The "Bleed" effect triggers. Radar goes fuzzy for 15 seconds.
- **10:00**: The Erebus stabilizes its position. Transit complete.

## Event Logic Sketch
- **Banshee Demo**: Command should prompt the player to use the Banshee on the Spires' shields.
- **SF Dragon Appearance**: Arjuna 1 is invulnerable and jumps out after 10 seconds of being targeted.

## New Ships/Weapons
- **GTW Banshee**: Introduced as the primary shield killer.

## Debriefing
- **Success**: We are in. The Void is... more vast than we imagined. The Erebus is holding together, but the crew is reporting auditory hallucinations. We need to find the GTI lab and get out of here.
- **Failure**: The Erebus was lost during the transit. The infection has claimed our finest ship and her crew. Sol is now undefended from the horrors within the Void.

## QA Notes
- The "red" background should be visually distinct but not so bright it obscures the HUD or Shivan ships (which are also dark).
- The "Bleed" hull damage should be a minor pressure, not a death sentence.
