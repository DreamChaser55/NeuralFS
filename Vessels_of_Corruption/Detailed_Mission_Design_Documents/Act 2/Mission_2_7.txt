# Mission Design Document: The Whispering Dark (2.7)

## Purpose and Player Experience
- **Mission Fantasy**: A terrifying game of hide-and-seek with a capital ship that seems alive. The player is vulnerable and overwhelmed by psychological horror.
- **Plot Advancement**: Discovery that the Shivan "Superdestroyers" (like the Lucifer) are not unique, and that the infection is a coordinated effort to "whisper" into human minds.
- **Tone**: Silent, breathless, and nightmarish.

## Environment and Setup
- **Datestamp**: 10 Nov 2335
- **System**: The Void
- **Location**: A deep "shadow pocket" where sensor visibility is near zero.
- **Backgrounds**: Pitch black with glowing purple veins in the distance. The SD Malphas (Demon-class) is moored to a colossal, translucent organic structure.
- **Hazards**: "Psychic Interference" – The player's HUD flickers with images of dead wingmen. Engine noise is replaced by whispering voices.

## Participants
- **Allied**:
    - **Alpha 1 (Player)**: 1x GTF Loki (Advanced Stealth).
- **Hostile/Neutral**:
    - **SD Malphas (Demon-class)**: The Target. Status: Pulsating. It has multiple "eye" sensors that can detect the player if they get too close or fly too fast.
    - **Shivan Dragon (SF Dragon)**: 2 wings of 2 (Rama and Krishna). They patrol in slow, rhythmic circles.
    - **Bio-Spires**: 10x Spires acting as a sensor net.

## Player Craft and Loadout
- **Default Ship**: GTF Loki (Stealth Variant).
- **Allowed Ships**: GTF Loki only.
- **Default Loadout**: No primary weapons (Disabled for stealth). No secondary weapons. (Focus on Scanning).

## Objectives and Directives
- **Primary**:
    - Scan the SD Malphas's 4 primary bio-ports.
    - Remain undetected by the Shivan patrol.
    - Identify the source of the "Whisper" signal (The "Node Anchor").
- **Secondary**:
    - Scan the moored organic structure.
- **Bonus**:
    - Get within 100 meters of the Malphas's bridge without being detected.

## Command Briefing
- **Stage 1**: The data from the Necromancer lab points to a massive Shivan presence in the Void's "Lower Reaches." We believe this is the command hub for the infection.
- **Stage 2**: Due to the density of the Shivan sensor net, we cannot send a wing. Alpha 1, you will go in alone using a specialized Loki with a reduced sensor profile.
- **Stage 3**: Your objective is to scan the SD Malphas, a Demon-class destroyer that appears to be the "brood mother" for the infection in this sector. Do not engage. If you are spotted, you will likely be destroyed before you can jump.

## Mission Briefing
- **Stage 1**: Alpha 1, the Malphas is anchored to a structure we've never seen before. It looks biological. Stay at low thrust; high engine output will flare their sensors.
- **Stage 2**: You need to scan the four bio-ports on the Malphas's hull. These are the points where the infection is being "uploaded" to the ship.
- **Stage 3**: The "Whisper" signal—the static we've been hearing—is strongest near the Malphas's core. Find the source. And Alpha 1... if you hear voices, ignore them. It's just sensor feedback.

## In-mission Communications
- **Alpha 1 (Player)**: (Silent).
- **Erebus Command (Distorted)**: "Alpha... 1... can you... hear... us? Sensors... failing..."
- **The Whisper**: (Soft voices) "Pilot... why do you hide? We see your fear. It is a beautiful... pattern. Let us... complete you."
- **Alpha 2 (Voice from the Past)**: "Is that you, Alpha 1? Why did you leave me at Ross 128? It's so cold here." (Note: This is an auditory hallucination).
- **Shivan Patrol (Rama 1)**: (Passes within 500m. Heartbeat sound intensifies in the player's headset).

## Event Flow and Timing
- **00:00**: Alpha 1 jumps in 6km from the Malphas. Total silence.
- **02:00**: Player must navigate the Bio-Spire sensor net. Moving > 35m/s triggers a warning.
- **04:00**: First bio-port scan. The port "blinks" like an eye when scanned.
- **06:00**: The "Whisper" intensifies. The HUD begins to show false Shivan signatures that disappear when approached.
- **08:00**: Final scan complete. The "Node Anchor" is revealed—a pulsating Shivan organ suspended in the center of the moored structure.
- **10:00**: Player jumps out just as the Malphas "wakes up" and lets out a massive energy flare.

## Event Logic Sketch
- **Stealth Logic**: Detection is based on distance (< 300m to any Shivan) and velocity (> 40m/s). If detected, all Shivans turn hostile and the mission becomes nearly impossible.
- **Hallucination Logic**: Images of destroyed allied ships (Fenris, Apollos) flicker on the screen for 0.5 seconds at random intervals.

## New Ships/Weapons
- **SD Malphas (Infected Demon)**: First appearance.

## Debriefing
- **Success**: You survived the belly of the beast. The data you brought back confirms our worst fears. The Malphas is a transmitter. It's sending the "Whisper" back to our fleet, preparing them for the harvest.
- **Failure**: Alpha 1 is gone. The Malphas continues its silent work. The "Whisper" is getting louder on the Erebus... I can hear it in my bunk.

## QA Notes
- The stealth mechanic must be fair; clear visual cues (the "eye" Spires) should show where the danger zones are.
- Hallucinations should be psychological, not gameplay-disrupting.
