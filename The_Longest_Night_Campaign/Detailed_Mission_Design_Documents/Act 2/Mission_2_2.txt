MISSION DESIGN DOCUMENT
Mission 2.2: "Blind Man's Bluff"

================================================================================
PURPOSE & EXPERIENCE
- Mission Fantasy: Stealth/Recon -> Assault.
- Tension: Identifying targets before being overwhelmed.
- Plot Advance: Finding the exit node to Beta Aquilae and clearing the defenses.

================================================================================
FICTION VIEWER (Intro)
"TACTICAL OFFICER'S REPORT: Lt. Cmdr. Sarah Jenks, GTD Stalwart.
Date: January 13, 2335.

We found it. A jump node.
It's unstable, fluctuating wildy, but it's a door out of this hellhole.
The problem is the doorman.
We've detected a massive Shivan presence around the node. Sentry guns. Fighters. And a sensor net that covers the entire grid.
If we warp the Stalwart in now, they'll tear us apart before we can stabilize the drive field.
We have to go in quietly. We have to blind them."

================================================================================
ENVIRONMENT & SETUP
- System: Laramis (Node Site).
- Background: Nebula.
- Objects:
  - Jump Node (Unstable).
  - Shivan Sentry Guns (Trident - Strong).
  - Shivan Sensor Array (Cargo containers/Comm nodes).

================================================================================
PARTICIPANTS
Allied:
- Alpha Wing (Player).
- GTT Argyll (Transport - deploying sensor buoys).

Hostile:
- Sentry Guns (6x Trident).
- Shivan Patrols (Scorpion, Manticore).
- Reinforcements (Basilisk).

================================================================================
PLAYER CRAFT
- Ship: GTF Loki (Stealth Recon - if available/applicable) or Valkyrie.
- Loadout: High speed, light weapons.

================================================================================
OBJECTIVES
Primary:
1. Scan Shivan Sensor Array (Determine Sentry Control).
2. Destroy Sentry Guns.
3. Defend GTT Argyll while it stabilizes the node.

Secondary:
- Destroy all Shivan Fighters.

================================================================================
BRIEFINGS

Mission Briefing:
1. "We've found it. A jump node. Calculations suggest it leads to Beta Aquilae."
2. "But the Shivans have it locked down. A perimeter of sentry guns and heavy fighter patrols."
3. "We can't take the Stalwart in until that path is clear."
4. "Alpha, you're going in first. We need you to blind them. Locate their sensor array and scan it."
5. "Then, take out those sentry guns. The Stalwart will be holding position until you give the all-clear."
6. "Expect heavy resistance once you start shooting."

================================================================================
IN-MISSION MESSAGES
- Arrival:
  - Alpha 2: "That's a lot of guns. They really don't want anyone using this door."
- Scanning:
  - Alpha 1: "Scanning array... done. Sending data."
  - Stalwart: "Good work. We've jammed their long-range comms. They can't call for capital ship support, but the locals are gonna be angry."
- Combat:
  - Sentry Guns activate.
  - Shivan patrols swarm.
- Node Stabilization:
  - Argyll: "Deploying stabilization buoys. This node is fluctuating wildy. It's going to be a rough ride."
- Climax:
  - Stalwart: "Argyll reports node is stable. Alpha, clear the remaining fighters. We are inbound."

================================================================================
EVENT FLOW
1. Player arrives. Avoids or engages initial patrol.
2. Player scans Sensor Array.
3. Sentry guns become fully active/hostile.
4. Player destroys Sentry Guns.
5. Argyll moves in to work on the node.
6. Heavier Shivan patrol (Basilisks) spawns to attack Argyll.
7. Player defends Argyll.
8. Mission complete.

================================================================================
DEBRIEFING
Success:
"The path is clear. The node is stable... enough.
We're going home, pilots.
Prepare for jump to Beta Aquilae.
Let's hope there's someone left on the other side to welcome us."
