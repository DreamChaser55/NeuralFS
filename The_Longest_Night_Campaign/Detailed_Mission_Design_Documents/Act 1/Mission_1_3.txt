MISSION DESIGN DOCUMENT
Mission 1.3: "Technological Terror"

================================================================================
PURPOSE & EXPERIENCE
- Mission Fantasy: The Heist. Stealing fire from the gods.
- Tension: Sneaking into a lion's den. The realization of what the cargo is.
- Plot Advance: Acquiring Shield Technology. This is the pivotal moment that upgrades the player from "victim" to "combatant."

================================================================================
FICTION VIEWER (Intro)
"ENGINEERING REPORT: Chief Engineer O'Brien.
Date: January 9, 2335.

We are holding it together with duct tape and prayers. The Stalwart took a beating at Ross 128. We have hull breaches on decks 4, 7, and 12.
But the biggest problem isn't the hull. It's the supplies. We are running low on tibanna gas for the reactors. We are running low on food.
Long-range scans picked up a cache. Shivan, by the look of it.
The Captain wants to raid it. I told him he's crazy.
He told me that without those supplies, we'll be drifting dead in a week.
So... we're going robbing."

================================================================================
ENVIRONMENT & SETUP
- System: Laramis (Deep Nebula).
- Background: Darker, denser nebula. Greenish tint (Shivan influence/gas).
- Objects:
  - Shivan Depot (3x Cargo Containers, 1x Sentry Gun "Eye of Sauron" equivalent).
  - Nav Buoys (Shivan markers).
  - SC Azrael (Transport) - Docked.

================================================================================
PARTICIPANTS
Allied:
- Alpha Wing (Player, 4x Apollo).
- GTT Argyll (Transport) - Boarding team.
- Beta Wing (Reinforcement).

Hostile:
- Depot Guards "Keeper" (2x Manticore).
- Reinforcements "Wrath" (2x Basilisk).
- Sentry Gun (Shivan Trident).

================================================================================
PLAYER CRAFT
- Ship: GTF Apollo.
- Loadout: ML-16, MX-50.

================================================================================
OBJECTIVES
Primary:
1. Destroy the Sentry Gun.
2. Scan the Cargo Containers (Identify "Shield Emitter" components).
3. Disable the SC Azrael (Optional/Bonus).
4. Protect GTT Argyll during docking/loading.
5. Escape with the cargo.

================================================================================
BRIEFINGS

Mission Briefing:
1. "We're running low on everything. Food, fuel, hope. But long-range scans have picked up a stationary signal."
2. "It's a supply depot. Shivan. If they have supplies, maybe they have something we can use."
3. "We need you to go in, blind the sentries, and scan those containers."
4. "The GTT Argyll is standing by with a boarding party. If you find anything useful... we take it."
5. "This is risky, Alpha. But if we don't resupply, we die here anyway."

================================================================================
IN-MISSION MESSAGES
- Arrival:
  - Alpha 2: "It's quiet. Too quiet."
  - Alpha 1: "Cut the chatter. Targets ahead."
- Scanning:
  - Computer: "Scan complete. Contents: High-energy field generators."
  - Stalwart (Comms): "Field generators? That sounds like... shields. Alpha, we need those containers. Bring in the Argyll."
- The Heist:
  - Argyll: "Docking sequence initiated. Cracking the hatch... jackpot. These look like miniaturized shield emitters."
- Counter-Attack:
  - Stalwart: "Incoming jump signatures! They're coming for their stuff!"
  - Wrath Wing arrives.
  - Argyll: "Loading 80%... 90%... We can't disconnect yet!"
- Departure:
  - Argyll: "Cargo secured! Get us out of here!"

================================================================================
EVENT FLOW
1. Player arrives. Destroys Sentry Gun (triggers "Keeper" wing activation if not stealth-killed quickly).
2. Player scans containers. Finds "Shield Tech".
3. GTT Argyll warps in, moves to dock.
4. "Wrath" wing arrives to intercept Argyll. Player must defend.
5. Argyll finishes loading, undocks, warps out.
6. Player warps out.

================================================================================
DEBRIEFING
Success:
"Outstanding work, Alpha.
The tech boys are going crazy over this cargo. It's shield technology.
They say they can retrofit our fighters within 24 hours.
It won't be perfect, but it will give us a fighting chance against those black ships.
You just leveled the playing field."

Failure (Argyll Destroyed):
"We lost the Argyll. And with it, our best chance at survival.
The Stalwart is blind, deaf, and defenseless.
Hope you're happy, pilot."
