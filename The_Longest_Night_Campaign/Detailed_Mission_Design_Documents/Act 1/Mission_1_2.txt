MISSION DESIGN DOCUMENT
Mission 1.2: "Ghosts in the Fog"

================================================================================
PURPOSE & EXPERIENCE
- Mission Fantasy: Submarine warfare in a nebula. Blind, paranoid, claustrophobic.
- Tension: High. Sensors are unreliable. The enemy is a "blip" before they are a visual contact.
- Plot Advance: Realizing Laramis is not empty; it's Shivan territory.

================================================================================
FICTION VIEWER (Intro)
"PILOT'S DIARY: Ensign 'Rook' Davis, Alpha 3.
Date: January 8, 2335.

I can't see the stars.
We jumped into... soup. It's thick, purple, and it messes with your head. The radar keeps pinging contacts that aren't there. We call them 'ghosts'.
The Captain says we're safe here. That the nebula hides us.
But if it hides us from them, it hides them from us.
I was on deck watch for four hours. I swear I saw something move out there. Something blacker than the space around it.
I'm not sleeping tonight."

================================================================================
ENVIRONMENT & SETUP
- System: Laramis (Nebula).
- Background: Thick purple/blue nebula fog. Visibility reduced (1000m).
- Objects:
  - Asteroids (scattered, providing false radar echoes).
  - Nav Buoy (deployed by Stalwart to mark the entry node).
  - Wreckage (Shivan Fighter - "Unknown Anomaly").

================================================================================
PARTICIPANTS
Allied:
- GTD Stalwart (Damaged, engines @ 30%).
- Alpha Wing (Player, 4x Apollo).
- Beta Wing (Reinforcements, 3x Valkyrie - if acquired, otherwise Apollo).

Hostile:
- Unknown Echoes (Stealth mechanics or asteroids).
- Shivan Scout Wing "Vanish" (2x Scorpion, 2x Manticore).
- Shivan Patrol "Spectre" (4x Basilisk).

================================================================================
PLAYER CRAFT
- Ship: GTF Apollo.
- Loadout: ML-16, MX-50.

================================================================================
OBJECTIVES
Primary:
1. Patrol the perimeter (Nav 1, Nav 2, Nav 3).
2. Investigate sensor anomalies.
3. Destroy Shivan scout wings.
4. Protect the Stalwart.

Secondary:
- Scan "Unknown Anomaly" (Wrecked Shivan Fighter).

================================================================================
BRIEFINGS

Mission Briefing:
1. "We have arrived in the Laramis system. Or at least, that's what the star charts say. It's a mess out there."
2. "The nebula is wreaking havoc on our sensors. We are effectively blind."
3. "Engineering is working on the jump drive, but we are sitting ducks until it's fixed."
4. "Alpha, fly a patrol pattern around the Stalwart. Investigate any contacts. We need to know if we were followed."
5. "Rules of engagement: Fire only if fired upon. We don't want to ring the dinner bell if we don't have to."

================================================================================
IN-MISSION MESSAGES
- Start:
  - Stalwart: "Alpha, sensors are showing... ghosts. Multiple contacts, fading in and out. Check them out."
- Contact 1 (Asteroid):
  - Alpha 2: "I have a lock! Engaging... wait. It's just a rock. Damn nebula."
- Contact 2 (Wreckage):
  - Alpha 1: "Found something. It's metal. Looks like... wreckage."
  - Stalwart: "Scan it."
  - Scan Complete: "It's alien. But it's dead. Looks like it crashed here a long time ago."
- Contact 3 (Shivan Scouts):
  - Alpha 1 (Player/Persona): "Contact! It's one of those black ships!"
  - Stalwart: "Identify! Is it the Lucifer?"
  - Alpha 1: "Negative. Small craft. Fighters."
- Combat:
  - Shivan ships use hit-and-run tactics, fading into the fog.
  - Stalwart: "We're picking up a transmission from those fighters. It's a directed burst... they're calling for help."
  - Objective Update: "Stop that transmission! Destroy them before they escape!"
- Climax:
  - A larger wing (Spectre) jumps in near the Stalwart.
  - Stalwart: "They found us! All batteries, free fire!"
- Aftermath:
  - Stalwart: "Clear. But they know we're here now. We need to move."

================================================================================
EVENT FLOW
1. Patrol Waypoints 1 & 2: Nothing but asteroids and static.
2. Waypoint 3: Sensor anomaly (Wreckage). Scanning adds lore entry.
3. Waypoint 4: Sensor anomaly. Spawns "Vanish" wing (Scorpions). They don't attack immediately; they observe.
4. If Player approaches < 500m OR fires: Vanish wing attacks.
5. If Vanish wing takes 50% losses: They attempt to jump out (flee).
6. Trigger: If Vanish wing escapes OR is destroyed, "Spectre" wing (Basilisks) arrives to attack the Stalwart.
7. Player must defeat Spectre wing.
8. Mission End.

================================================================================
DEBRIEFING
Success:
"Good shooting, Alpha.
We stopped their scouts, but the element of surprise is gone.
We've analyzed the debris. These ships are... efficient. No life support. Minimal crew quarters.
They are built for one thing: war.
We have found a stable orbit in a nearby asteroid cluster. We're moving the Stalwart there for repairs."

Partial (Scouts Escaped):
"You let the scouts escape, pilot.
They are undoubtedly reporting our position to their main fleet right now.
We have to move, and fast. The repairs will have to wait."
