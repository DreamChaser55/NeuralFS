MISSION DESIGN DOCUMENT
Mission 1.4: "The Flail"

================================================================================
PURPOSE & EXPERIENCE
- Mission Fantasy: Indiana Jones in space. Discovery.
- Tension: Navigating a dangerous environment (Asteroids) while under fire.
- Plot Advance: Finding the Flail prototype (connecting to FS1 lore).

================================================================================
FICTION VIEWER (Intro)
"SCIENTIFIC LOG: Dr. Aris Thorne, GTT Argyll.
Date: January 11, 2335.

I don't believe it. I simply don't believe it.
The signals we intercepted from the moon... they are Terran. But they are old. Decades old.
The encryption matches the pre-war GTI protocols. This wasn't a colony. It was a black site.
A secret weapons lab, hidden out here where no one would look.
What were they building? And why did they stop?
We are going down to find out. If the Shivans don't kill us first."

================================================================================
ENVIRONMENT & SETUP
- System: Laramis (Asteroid Field).
- Background: Lighter nebula, filled with rocks.
- Objects:
  - Ruined Installation (Terran/Ancient hybrid - mysterious).
  - Asteroid Field (Dense).

================================================================================
PARTICIPANTS
Allied:
- Alpha Wing (Player, 4x Valkyrie - Shielded!).
- GTT Argyll (Transport) - Science team.
- Beta Wing (Bombers).

Hostile:
- Shivan "Guardian" Wing (4x Basilisk).
- Shivan "Hunter" Wing (2x Manticore).

================================================================================
PLAYER CRAFT
- Ship: GTF Valkyrie (Newly retrofitted with shields).
- Loadout: ML-16, MX-50.

================================================================================
OBJECTIVES
Primary:
1. Escort GTT Argyll to the Ruin.
2. Defend Argyll during extraction.
3. Destroy Shivan interceptors.

Secondary:
- Destroy all large asteroids in the Argyll's flight path.

================================================================================
BRIEFINGS

Mission Briefing:
1. "The shields are installed and holding. Good work."
2. "We've detected a metallic signature on a moon in the outer belt. It's not Shivan."
3. "It matches old GTA transponder codes... codes that haven't been used in 50 years."
4. "The Argyll is going down to investigate. Alpha, you're on escort duty."
5. "Watch those asteroids. And watch for our friends in black."

================================================================================
IN-MISSION MESSAGES
- Arrival:
  - Alpha 1: "Look at that ruin. That's Human architecture."
  - Argyll: "Confirming. It's a GTI listening post. Abandoned. No... destroyed."
- Discovery:
  - Argyll: "We found something. A weapon schematic. And a prototype. It's... crude. But powerful."
  - Stalwart: "Bring it up. We can analyze it on the ship."
- Combat:
  - Shivan "Guardian" wing jumps in.
  - Alpha 2: "Shields holding! It's good to not be naked out here anymore."
  - Combat ensues.
- Departure:
  - Argyll: "Package secured. Returning to base."

================================================================================
EVENT FLOW
1. Escort Argyll through asteroid field (Player must shoot asteroids to clear path).
2. Argyll docks with ruin (simulated by hovering near wreckage).
3. "Guardian" wing attacks.
4. "Hunter" wing attacks (flanking).
5. Argyll extracts and departs.

================================================================================
DEBRIEFING
Success:
"The object you retrieved is a kinetic energy weapon. The 'Flail'.
It seems GTI was experimenting with shield-piercing tech out here decades ago.
Irony is, they never found a use for it. Until now.
We can manufacture ammo for it. It should help punch through those Shivan hulls."
