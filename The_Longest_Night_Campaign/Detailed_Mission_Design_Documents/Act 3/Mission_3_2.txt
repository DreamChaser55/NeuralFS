MISSION DESIGN DOCUMENT
Mission 3.2: "Morning's Light"

================================================================================
PURPOSE & EXPERIENCE
- Mission Fantasy: Last Stand.
- Tension: Defending a crippled ship against waves of enemies. Relief when allies arrive.
- Plot Advance: Rejoining the GTA. Handing over the intel.

================================================================================
FICTION VIEWER (Intro)
"EPILOGUE: From the Personal Files of Admiral Wolf, GTD Galatea.
Date: January 15, 2335.

We were losing hope. The losses in Antares were staggering. The Vasudan front was collapsing.
And then, out of the darkness... a ghost ship appeared in Beta Aquilae.
The Stalwart.
Burned, battered, venting atmosphere... but alive.
They brought with them more than just their lives. They brought maps. They brought weapons tech. They brought hope.
They say they walked through the valley of the shadow of death.
Looking at their hull... I believe them."

================================================================================
ENVIRONMENT & SETUP
- System: Beta Aquilae.
- Background: Standard Starfield (Bright, hopeful).
- Objects:
  - GTD Stalwart (Crippled, venting plasma).
  - Beta Aquilae Installation (Distant - destination).
  - GTC Orff (Fenris - arrives late).

================================================================================
PARTICIPANTS
Allied:
- Alpha Wing (Player).
- GTD Stalwart.
- Reinforcements: GTD Galatea / GTC Orff (Scripted arrival).

Hostile:
- Shivan Pursuit Force (SC Tenebris - if it survived earlier, or a new Lilith).
- Shivan Wings (All types).

================================================================================
PLAYER CRAFT
- Ship: Any available.
- Loadout: Any.

================================================================================
OBJECTIVES
Primary:
1. Defend GTD Stalwart.
2. Destroy Shivan Pursuit Force.
3. Survive until reinforcements arrive.

Secondary:
- Keep Stalwart Hull > 20%.

================================================================================
BRIEFINGS

Mission Briefing:
1. "We've arrived in Beta Aquilae. But the jump drive is fused. We are dead in the water."
2. "We're hailing the Beta Aquilae Installation, but no response yet."
3. "Sensors show the Shivans have followed us through. A massive force."
4. "We have no main battery power. We are sitting ducks."
5. "Alpha, you are the only thing standing between us and destruction. Hold them off until help arrives."
6. "This is the end of the line, pilots. Make it count."

================================================================================
IN-MISSION MESSAGES
- Start:
  - Stalwart: "Mayday, Mayday! This is GTD Stalwart. We are under attack by Shivan forces! Is anyone out there?"
  - Radio Static.
  - Alpha 2: "Here they come! Fighters, 12 o'clock!"
- Combat:
  - Waves of Shivan fighters attack.
  - Cruiser arrives (Tenebris?).
  - Stalwart: "Hull critical! We can't take much more of this!"
- The Turn:
  - Radio: "Stalwart, this is the GTD Galatea. We read you. Hang on, we are en route."
  - Alpha 1: "The Galatea! We're saved!"
  - Galatea jumps in with full fighter escort.
  - Galatea: "All batteries, target that Shivan cruiser. Fire!"
- Victory:
  - Shivan forces destroyed/retreat.
  - Galatea: "Welcome back to the land of the living, Stalwart. We thought you were dead."
  - Stalwart: "Not yet, Admiral. Not yet. We have intel... you're going to want to see this."

================================================================================
EVENT FLOW
1. Stalwart is stationary.
2. Shivan fighters attack in waves (3-4 mins).
3. Shivan Cruiser jumps in.
4. Player must disarm cruiser or distract bombers.
5. GTD Galatea jumps in (Massive firepower).
6. Galatea destroys Shivan cruiser.
7. Mission End.

================================================================================
DEBRIEFING
Success:
"The Stalwart is safe. The data on the Laramis node and the Flail weapon has been transferred to Command.
You've done the impossible, pilot. You brought them home.
The war isn't over. The Lucifer is still out there.
But for tonight... we celebrate.
Dismissed."
