MISSION DESIGN DOCUMENT
Mission 3.1: "The Gauntlet"

================================================================================
PURPOSE & EXPERIENCE
- Mission Fantasy: Trench run / Blockade run.
- Tension: High speed, constant fire.
- Plot Advance: The final dash to the exit.

================================================================================
FICTION VIEWER (Intro)
"CAPTAIN'S SPEECH: Captain Vance, GTD Stalwart.
Date: January 14, 2335.

(Broadcast to all decks)
This is the Captain speaking.
In a few moments, we will begin our run to the Beta Aquilae jump node.
We are tired. We are damaged. We are low on ammo.
But we are not beaten.
The Shivans have thrown everything they have at us. Nebulas, mines, cruisers... and we are still here.
There is a blockade ahead. Two cruisers. A wall of fighters.
They think they can stop us.
Let's prove them wrong.
Action stations. All wings, launch.
Let's go home."

================================================================================
ENVIRONMENT & SETUP
- System: Laramis (Exit Node).
- Background: Nebula clearing up (starfield visible?).
- Objects:
  - Jump Node (Beta Aquilae).
  - Shivan Blockade Force (Cruisers, Freighters).

================================================================================
PARTICIPANTS
Allied:
- GTD Stalwart (Moving to node).
- Alpha Wing (Player).
- Beta Wing (Bombers).
- GTT Argyll (Transport).

Hostile:
- SC Kali (Cain Cruiser) - Blocking left flank.
- SC Durga (Cain Cruiser) - Blocking right flank.
- Shivan Fighter Wings (Manticore, Basilisk, Scorpion).

================================================================================
PLAYER CRAFT
- Ship: GTF Hercules (for durability) or Valkyrie.
- Loadout: Flail, Heavy Missiles.

================================================================================
OBJECTIVES
Primary:
1. Destroy SC Kali.
2. Destroy SC Durga.
3. Protect GTD Stalwart.
4. Escort Stalwart to Node.

Secondary:
- Destroy all Shivan Fighters.

================================================================================
BRIEFINGS

Command Briefing (Act 3 Intro):
"We are at the threshold.
Behind us lies the nebula. Ahead, the Beta Aquilae system. And hopefully, the Alliance.
But the Shivans aren't letting us leave without a fight.
Sensors show a blockade force at the node. Two cruisers. Heavy fighter cover.
This is it. We punch through, or we die here."

Mission Briefing:
1. "The Stalwart is beginning its run to the node. Speed is set to maximum."
2. "Two Cain-class cruisers are blocking the vector. They will tear the Stalwart apart if they aren't neutralized."
3. "Alpha, Beta... clear the path. Focus fire on the cruisers. Strip their turrets, then their hulls."
4. "Ignore the fighters if you can. The cruisers are the priority."
5. "Once the Stalwart is through, follow us. Do not get left behind."

================================================================================
IN-MISSION MESSAGES
- Start:
  - Stalwart: "All hands to battle stations. This is the final run. Void Walkers, clear the road."
  - Alpha 1: "We're on it. Beta, form up on me."
- Combat:
  - SC Kali/Durga open fire.
  - Stalwart: "Taking hits! Forward shields down to 50%!"
  - Alpha 2: "That cruiser is down! One more to go!"
- Shivan Reinforcements:
  - Computer: "Incoming jump signatures. More fighters!"
  - Stalwart: "We can't stop! Keep them off us!"
- Departure:
  - Stalwart: "We are at the node! Engaging jump drive... NOW!"
  - Alpha 1: "Stalwart is away! Let's get out of here!"

================================================================================
EVENT FLOW
1. Stalwart moves toward node (slowly).
2. Kali and Durga engage Stalwart.
3. Player/Beta attack cruisers.
4. Heavy fighter resistance spawns.
5. Stalwart reaches node and jumps.
6. Player jumps.

================================================================================
DEBRIEFING
Success:
"We made it.
The nebula is behind us. The stars... the stars look different here.
Beta Aquilae.
We are back in Terran space.
But we're not safe yet. We're picking up distress signals from the local garrison.
The war followed us home."

================================================================================
QA NOTES
- Timing: Ensure the Stalwart has enough health to survive the run provided the player is efficient.
- Cruiser balance: Two Cains is manageable with bomber support.
