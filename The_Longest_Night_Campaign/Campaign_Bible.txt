Campaign: The Longest Night
Author: Roo-FS

================================================================================
CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW
================================================================================

CONCEPT
During the initial Shivan incursion at Ross 128 (2335), the GTD Stalwart (Orion-class) is cut off from the main GTA fleet. With the Delta Serpentis node blockaded by the SD Lucifer, the Stalwart is forced to jump into an unstable, uncharted node leading to the Laramis system. The campaign chronicles the Stalwart's desperate journey through Laramis—a nebular system teeming with Shivan listening posts—to find a "back door" into Allied space (Beta Aquilae). This campaign canonizes the discovery of the "Flail" weapon and the Laramis route mentioned in the FS1 Universe Bible.

TIMELINE
January 7, 2335 - January 15, 2335
(Runs parallel to FS1 Act 1. Ends just before the Galatea moves to Beta Aquilae).

FACTIONS & ORDER OF BATTLE

Terran (GTA) - Task Force Stalwart
- GTD Stalwart (Orion Destroyer) - Flagship. Old, reliable, carrying the fleet's hopes.
- GTC Crecy (Fenris Cruiser) - Escort. (To be destroyed in Act 2).
- GTT Argyll (Elysium Transport) - Carrying civilian contractors/scientists.
- Alpha Wing ("Void Walkers"): Player squadron (Apollo/Valkyrie).
- Beta Wing ("Iron Maidens"): Bomber/Heavy support (Athena/Hercules).

Shivans
- SC Tenebris (Lilith Cruiser) - The persistent hunter tracking the Stalwart.
- Various patrol wings (Scorpion, Manticore, Basilisk).
- Shivan logistics vessels (Azrael, Mephisto).

RULES & CONSTRAINTS (FS1 Era)
- Shields: Terran fighters have no shields initially, but Shivans do. We will assume the Stalwart acquires shield tech during the campaign (scavenged). *Decision*: Player starts Unshielded (Tension!). Mission 3 involves stealing Shivan shield emitters from a depot, allowing retrofits for Mission 4 onwards.
- Weapons: ML-16, Disruptor, Flail (Prototype discovered in Laramis). Missiles: Fury, MX-50.
- Subspace: No shields in subspace. Inter-system jumps require nodes.

THEMES
- Isolation: No reinforcements. Every fighter lost is gone forever.
- The Unknown: Laramis is a foggy, sensor-obscuring nightmare.
- Desperation: Objectives are about survival (fuel, food, ammo), not winning the war.
- Sacrifice: The burden of command and the cost of bringing the crew home.

================================================================================
MISSION LIST
================================================================================

ACT 1: INTO THE ABYSS
Focus: The initial ambush and the desperate jump into the unknown.

Mission 1: "Burning Skies"
- System: Ross 128
- Date: 1/07/2335
- Situation: The Stalwart is investigating the disappearance of the GTSC Einstein near an unstable anomalous jump node.
- Event: The SD Lucifer arrives and obliterates the GTI Riviera. The Stalwart is too far to help but is spotted by Shivan vanguard elements.
- Objective: Defend the Stalwart from Shivan bombers while the jump drive charges. The Delta Serpentis node is blocked by the Lucifer. With numbers of Shivan jump signatures inbound, Captain orders a blind jump through the unstable jump node (leading to Laramis).
- Atmosphere: Panic, overwhelming odds.
- Fiction Viewer: Captain Vance's Log - The monotony of patrol before the chaos.

Mission 2: "Ghosts in the Fog"
- System: Laramis (Nebula)
- Date: 1/08/2335
- Situation: The fleet arrives in Laramis. Sensors are fuzzy. The Stalwart's drive is damaged.
- Event: Recon mission. Player encounters "ghost" echoes on radar (Shivan stealth patrols and debris). Discovery of a wrecked Shivan fighter foreshadows their presence.
- Objective: Locate a safe anchorage for repairs. Destroy a Shivan scout wing before they can report the fleet's position.
- Twist: The nebula interferes with comms; player is isolated from the Stalwart for a tense 2 minutes.
- Fiction Viewer: Pilot's Diary - The psychological toll of the silence and the "ghosts" on sensors.

Mission 3: "Technological Terror"
- System: Laramis
- Date: 1/09/2335
- Situation: The Stalwart needs supplies to repair the jump drive. Long-range scans detect a Shivan supply depot.
- Event: Raid the depot.
- Objective: Steal Shivan cargo containers (Shield components). Defend the GTT Argyll during the loading operation.
- Payoff: Acquiring shield technology.
- Fiction Viewer: Chief Engineer's Report - The desperate need for supplies and the hope these strange containers bring.

ACT 2: THE LONG DARK
Focus: Survival and discovery in Laramis.

Mission 4: "The Flail"
- System: Laramis (Asteroid Field)
- Date: 1/11/2335
- Situation: A science team from the Argyll investigates a ruined installation on a moon (Ancient or early Terran fail?).
- Event: They find a weapon prototype (The Flail) left by a previous, lost expedition or Ancient cache. It was a GTI "Black Site" hiding from the GTA.
- Objective: Escort the GTT Argyll back to the Stalwart while under heavy attack by Shivan Basilisks.
- Mechanic: Asteroid field navigation.
- Fiction Viewer: Science Officer's Note - The mystery of the ruins and the "Flail" weapon's potential.

Mission 5: "Sacrifice"
- System: Laramis
- Date: 1/12/2335
- Situation: The SC Tenebris (Lilith-class) has found the fleet.
- Event: The Stalwart is not ready to jump. The GTC Crecy offers to draw the Tenebris away.
- Objective: Disable the Tenebris's engines to buy time. Cover the Crecy's retreat and then escort its escape pods as it is destroyed.
- Tone: Tragic. The player wins the objective but loses the cruiser.
- Fiction Viewer: Commander Haddock's Final Log - Acceptance of duty and the decision to stand and fight.

Mission 6: "Blind Man's Bluff"
- System: Laramis
- Date: 1/13/2335
- Situation: The Stalwart is running on fumes. They detect a jump node signature, but it's guarded by a Shivan fighters and sentry guns with a sensor array.
- Event: Stealth/Sensor mission.
- Objective: Navigate the node area, scan the sensor array, tag the sentry guns for the Stalwart's long-range artillery, and clear the path to the node.
- Mechanic: Stay outside sentry detection range until tagged.
- Fiction Viewer: Stalwart Tactical Officer - The tension of the upcoming operation and the hope of escape.

ACT 3: THE WAY HOME
Focus: Punching through the blockade back to Terran space.

Mission 7: "The Gauntlet"
- System: Laramis -> Beta Aquilae Node
- Date: 1/14/2335
- Situation: The fleet moves to the exit node.
- Event: A large Shivan blockade force (Two cruisers and wings) stands between the Stalwart and the node.
- Objective: A "corridor of death" run. Destroy the blocking cruisers (Cain-class). Ensure Stalwart reaches the node.
- Fiction Viewer: Captain Vance's Speech - A final rally for the crew before the desperate run.

Mission 8: "Morning's Light"
- System: Beta Aquilae
- Date: 1/15/2335
- Situation: The Stalwart jumps into Beta Aquilae, shocking the local GTA garrison who thought them dead.
- Event: The Shivans follow them through.
- Objective: The Stalwart is venting plasma and has no weapons power. Player must defend the helpless destroyer against the pursuing Shivan strike force until the GTD Galatea (or local garrison) arrives to save them.
- Ending: The Stalwart arrives at Beta Aquilae Installation. The data on Laramis and the Flail is handed over.
- Fiction Viewer: Epilogue - The cost of survival and the preparation for the continued war.
