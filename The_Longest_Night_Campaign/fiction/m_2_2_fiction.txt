TACTICAL OFFICER'S REPORT: Lt. Cmdr. Sarah Jenks, GTD Stalwart.
Date: January 13, 2335.

We found it. A jump node.
It's unstable, fluctuating wildy, but it's a door out of this hellhole.
The problem is the doorman.
We've detected a massive Shivan presence around the node. Sentry guns. Fighters. And a sensor net that covers the entire grid.
If we warp the Stalwart in now, they'll tear us apart before we can stabilize the drive field.
We have to go in quietly. We have to blind them.
